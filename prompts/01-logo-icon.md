# Logo Icon (Symbol Only)

## Purpose
The core brand mark for MCP Dashboard. Used as app icon, favicon source, avatar, and anywhere the brand needs representation without text. This symbol represents the convergence of automated testing precision and AI-powered intelligence—the moment where test data transforms into actionable insights.

## Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions**: 512 x 512 px
- **Background**: Solid #0D0F14 (Deep Void) for easy removal
- **Format**: PNG (for transparency after background removal)
- **Style**: Minimalist geometric, cosmic, luminous, premium tech

## Image Generation Prompt

```
A striking abstract geometric logo centered on a solid dark background color #0D0F14. The symbol depicts "The Convergence Lens" - three elegant curved lines in electric indigo #6366F1 that spiral inward like orbital paths, converging toward a central luminous core. At the center, a radiant amber #E5A832 sphere glows with soft inner light, appearing to bend the indigo paths around it like gravitational lensing. The amber core has a subtle golden gradient from #E5A832 at center to #D4952B at edges. A single thin white #FFFFFF arc crosses through the composition at a precise 23-degree angle, representing the calibration mark of testing precision. The indigo orbital lines have a subtle glass-like quality with soft highlights. The overall effect suggests intelligence focusing data streams into clarity. Minimalist, geometric, vector-style, no texture, clean edges, professional tech aesthetic, suitable for app icon, the symbol floats on the solid #0D0F14 background with no gradients or patterns in the background itself.
```

## Style Notes
- **Central Core**: Amber sphere represents AI intelligence and the "passing state" - warmth, success, insight
- **Orbital Paths**: Three indigo curves represent test data streams being drawn toward analysis
- **Calibration Arc**: White line adds precision and prevents the symbol from feeling too organic
- **Gravitational Effect**: The way paths bend around the core suggests AI "pulling in" data for analysis
- **No symmetry**: Slight asymmetry makes it feel dynamic and alive, not static

## What to Avoid
- No gears, circuits, or mechanical elements
- No literal representation of tests (checkmarks, bugs, code)
- No hexagonal shapes
- No brain or neural imagery
- No generic tech globe or shield shapes

## Consistency Reference
This icon establishes the core visual language: amber warmth (AI/success) + indigo precision (tech/data) + white accents (clarity/calibration). All other assets derive from this symbol's DNA. The "convergence lens" concept—data bending toward intelligent analysis—should echo through the entire brand.
