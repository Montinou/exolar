# Default User Avatar

## Purpose
Placeholder avatar for users without profile photos. Represents team members in the dashboard—QA engineers, DevOps professionals, developers embracing AI-powered testing. Should feel friendly and professional while maintaining brand identity. Will be displayed in circular masks throughout the UI.

## Technical Specifications
- **Dimensions**: 256 x 256 px
- **Background**: Solid #1A1D24 (Cosmic Black) for easy removal
- **Format**: PNG (for transparency after background removal)
- **Style**: Abstract geometric, cosmic silhouette, minimal, approachable

## Image Generation Prompt

```
An abstract minimalist representation of a person on a solid background color #1A1D24. The figure is composed of simple geometric shapes: a perfect circle for the head rendered as a soft gradient from muted indigo #6366F1 at the top fading to the background color at the bottom, giving an ethereal floating quality. Below, a gentle curved arc suggesting shoulders, rendered in subtle amber #E5A832 with very low opacity, almost like a distant glow. Three tiny white #FFFFFF dots arranged in a slight arc above the head circle, like distant stars or data points orbiting the figure. The overall impression is a cosmic silhouette—a person made of light and data rather than flesh. Centered composition designed for circular cropping. Extremely minimal, only 4-5 visual elements total. Soft, diffused edges where colors meet background. No facial features, no detailed anatomy, purely abstract and geometric. Professional yet warm, suggesting intelligence and approachability. The solid #1A1D24 background has no texture or gradient.
```

## Style Notes
- **Abstraction Level**: Human form suggested through minimal geometry, not depicted literally
- **Cosmic Quality**: The figure feels like it exists in space—made of light, not solid
- **Gender Neutral**: Completely abstract to represent any team member
- **Warmth**: The subtle amber glow adds approachability to the tech aesthetic
- **Data Connection**: The orbiting dots subtly reference the dashboard's data-centric nature

## What to Avoid
- No realistic human features (eyes, nose, mouth)
- No gendered silhouettes or hairstyles
- No robot faces or AI stereotypes
- No busy patterns or multiple colors
- No hard edges—everything should feel soft and ethereal

## Consistency Reference
Uses the same color palette as the main logo (indigo + amber + white) but in a much more subtle, muted application. The "cosmic" and "light-based" quality connects to the broader brand theme of intelligence emerging from data. When displayed at small sizes (32-48px), only the head circle and faint glow should be visible.
